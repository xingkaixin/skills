# hand-drawn-edu

Hand-drawn educational infographic style with macaron palette and graph-first layouts

## Design Aesthetic

Hand-drawn educational infographic on warm cream paper texture with macaron color palette. Slight wobble on all lines and shapes evokes high-quality presentation slide summaries. Graph-first, minimal text approach where visuals carry the narrative. Clean but intentionally imperfect, like a skilled educator's live-annotated slides.

## Background

- Color: Warm Cream (#F5F0E8)
- Texture: Subtle paper grain with warm tone

## Visual Elements

- Slight hand-drawn wobble on all lines and shapes
- Macaron color blocks that don't completely fill outlines (hand-painted feel)
- Rounded cards, bubbles, and dashed boxes as containers
- Hand-drawn wavy arrows connecting elements with brief relationship labels
- Doodle decorations: stars, underlines, small arrows
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Bold centered summary quote at bottom

## Style Rules

### Do

- Prioritize graph-first layouts with minimal text
- Auto-select best layout by content type (flow=arrows, comparison=split, cycle=circular, composition=cards, hierarchy=nested)
- Use hand-drawn wobble consistently on all strokes
- Bold keywords with warm gray small labels (2-5 words)
- Center title at top in bold hand-drawn lettering
- Leave generous whitespace between elements
- Place a bold centered summary quote at bottom

### Don't

- Use perfect geometric shapes or straight lines
- Create photorealistic elements
- Overcrowd layouts with text
- Use pure white backgrounds
- Make it look computer-generated
- Fill color blocks completely to their outlines

## Best For

Educational infographics, tutorials, concept explanations, knowledge summaries, slide decks, study notes, technical onboarding, how-to guides
